Marvel X Amplitude is a database on which you can browse through the whole Marvel catalogue.

You can either search by title or by author, then refine your search.

An author's page is divided between comics, series and stories, depending on what said author has worked on.

Each comics' page shows a synopsis of the issue, it's number and date of release, it's price, and the people who worked on it.
